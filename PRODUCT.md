# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

General public — whoever finds her. No assumed prior relationship or niche audience. A stranger should feel like they know Desi after one post.

## Product Purpose

A personal blog where Desi shares what's on her mind: posts, photos, life. Not a platform or a brand — just her. Success is warmth and recognition, not traffic or conversion.

## Brand Personality

Honest. Open. Strong. A little wacky — not performed, just her. Subtle theatrical energy in the pacing and rhythm, never in the costume. Theater is an influence, not a metaphor.

## Anti-references

- Generic Squarespace / template blogs
- Slick lifestyle or fashion magazine aesthetic
- Literal theater: no playbills, curtains, spotlights, marquee fonts
- Overly polished personal brands

## Design Principles

1. **Personal over polished** — roughness and authenticity beat slickness every time
2. **Warmth before beauty** — the site should feel like a welcome, not an aesthetic statement
3. **Subtle drama** — theater energy lives in pacing, scale, and rhythm, not decoration
4. **Let the writing lead** — design steps back when there's content, forward when there isn't
5. **Earn the wackiness** — unexpected moments feel like Desi, not a design choice

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. No reduced-motion assumptions.
